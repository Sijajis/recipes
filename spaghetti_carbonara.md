# Spaghetti Carbonara

## Ingredients
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta
- 2 large eggs
- 50g pecorino cheese
- 50g parmesan
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Salt

## Instructions
1. Boil the spaghetti.
2. Fry the pancetta until crisp.
3. Beat the eggs and mix with the cheese.
4. Combine everything together with some pasta water.
